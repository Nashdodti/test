Ambitious plans require a specific approach.
That's why we focus on 3 complementary fields of expertise.

Software
Development

Lunar defines, designs and develops digital instruments that boost your business processes. We build intuitive intranets, custom portals, CRM-integrations and API development.

Our iterative process and continuous growth-mindset allows for small projects to scale efficiently and larger teams to transition smoothly towards innovative digital solutions.

  • Feasibility & Prototypes
  • UX & Interface design
  • Web-app development
  • Native development

Website
development

Web-technology is at Lunar's core expertise. We create visually unique and user-friendly websites for companies with growth ambitions. We align industry context, business objectives with bespoke design and a mobile first mindset.

We maximise efficiency by building on top on of
Craft CMS, providing teams with flexibility, ease of use and advanced power-options. Next to Craft CMS we build projects in Wordpress CMS and Shopify.

  • Website Development
  • User Experience Design
  • Conversion focussed website

Native app development

Lunar designs, develops and maintains your app presence in the various app-stores. We focus on user-flows and micro-interactions that stimulate certain behaviour. Our interactive prototypes help preview and test assumptions beforehand.

Our apps convert users and help businesses retain attention.

  • iOS App development
  • Android App development
  • App-API integrations
