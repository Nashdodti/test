Joining Lunar?

Working at Lunar is not your everyday job. Our values explain the way we work and what we expect from each team member.

  • You are highly motivated. You want to make progress in your career and develop yourself within a work hard, play hard mindset. Perhaps you want to become a team lead one day?
  • You are able to work independently and constantly think with a solution-focused mindset.
  • You value technical excellence and user experience.
  • You know everything about hard deadlines, but you have the experience to meet them in a relaxed spirit.
  • You interact with the other Lunars and share ideas in a convincing and constructive way.

Our offer

On top of a contract for full time employment and a competitive salary, we offer various other perks

  • A challenging job with flexible hours
  • All the devices and gadgets you need to create amazing work
  • The best colleagues in the world (and we’re not even exaggerating)
  • Office-spaces in Antwerp
  • Regular team-exercises
  • € 1 000 skill-building budget
  • Annual national and international team buildings
